About

How this website came about

In the summer of 2020, I had just finished my university degree and was spending the summer at home in Germany.
I always wanted to learn more Chengyu, as a way to improve the sophistication of my Mandarin. But at the same time, I also wanted to pick up some new skills, ideally related to programming or web development. Put two and two together, and so the idea of hosting a website to learn Chengyu and Web Development at the same time was born.

Many thanks go to Zhenghong (lieuzhenghong.com) who has helped me coming up with ideas and the necessary implementation to make this Website possible.

If you have comments, feedback or want to contribute to the collection of Chengyu, please feel free to reach out to me! You can reach me under: celine.li(at)gmx.net
