夜 yè 雨 yǔ 寄 jì 北 běi

Hover over the characters to see the pinyin!

Original Text

君问归期未有期，
巴山夜雨涨秋池。
何当共剪西窗烛，
却话巴山夜雨时。

Modern Chinese Translation

妻子问我什么时候回家， 但是我也不知道。
秋天的夜晚里， 巴山的雨下的不停，山边的水池的水涨得越来越高。
什么时候才能可以一起坐在西边的窗户剪蜡烛芯？
再和你说当时我在巴山的夜雨里给你写这封信的情景吧。

Pinyin

jūn wèn guī qī wèi yǒu qī
bā shān yè yǔ zhāng qiū chí
hé dāng gòng jiǎn xī chuāng zhú
què huà bā shān yè yǔ shí

English Translation

Sending North in a Rainy Night

You ask me, when I come home, but I do not know myself,
Rain falls heavily in this autumn night, the water in the lake next to the Ba Mountain is getting higher.
When we sit together again by the West Window to cut the candlewick,
I will tell you everything from tonight, the rain and the Ba Mountain.
