夜 yè 雨 yǔ 寄 jì 北 běi

Hover over the characters to see the pinyin!

Original Text

君问归期未有期，
巴山夜雨涨秋池。
何当共剪西窗烛，
却话9巴山夜雨时。

Breaking it down

Modern Chinese Translation

妻子问我什么时候回家， 但是我也不知道。
秋天的夜晚里， 巴山的雨下的不停，山边的水池的水涨得越来越高。
什么时候才能可以一起坐在西边的窗户剪蜡烛芯？
再和你说当时我在巴山的夜雨里给你写这封信的情景吧。

English Translation

Sending North in a Rainy Night You ask me, when I come home, but I do not know myself, Rain falls heavily in this autmn night, the water in the lake next to the Ba Mountain is getting higher. When we sit together again by the West Window to cut the candlewick, I will tell you everything from tonight, the rain and teh Ba Mountain.
