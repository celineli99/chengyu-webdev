见 jiàn 多 duō 识 shí 广 guǎng

Hover over the characters to see the pinyin!

English Translation

lit.: Seeing a lot will broaden your horizon and knowledge
Someone who is experienced and knowledgeable.

Breaking it down

  1. 见: See
  2. 多: Much
  3. 识: To know
  4. 广: broad, vast, extensive
