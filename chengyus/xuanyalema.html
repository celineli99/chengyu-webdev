悬 xuán 崖 yá 勒 lè 马 mǎ

Hover over the characters to see the pinyin!

English Translation

lit. to rein in the horse at the edge of the cliff
fig. wake up to danger in the last possible moment

Breaking it down

  1. 悬: to hang
  2. 崖: cliff
  3. 勒: to rein in
  4. 马: horse
