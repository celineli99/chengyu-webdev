望 wàng 子 zǐ 成 chéng 龙 lóng

Hover over the characters to see the pinyin!

English Translation

lit.: to hope one's son becomes a dragon
fig.: to long for one's children to succeed in life, to have great hopes for one's offspring

Breaking it down

  1. 望: to hope
  2. 子: children
  3. 成: to become
  4. 龙: dragon

Source: Google Image Search

Usage

家长要有望子成龙的愿望，更要有培养和教育孩子成材的行动。
