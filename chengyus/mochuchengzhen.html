磨 mó 杵 chǔ 成 chéng 针 zhēn

Hover over the characters to see the pinyin!

English Translation

lit.: an iron rod can be grinded into a needle
fig.: perseverance leads to success.

Breaking it down

  1. 磨: to grind
  2. 杵: iron rod
  3. 成: to become
  4. 针: needle

Usage

没有磨杵成针的毅力，学问上就不可能有很深的造诣.
